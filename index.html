We bring together programmers, IT specialists, designers, and strategists to create applications and other technological solutions. We welcome volunteers with different skills and different levels of experience, encouraging more-experienced volunteers to support those with less experience.

Our Values

We believe that:

  • non-profits are a much-needed force for change
  • technology can help non-profits achieve their goals more quickly and maximize their efforts
  • people in Montreal's tech community care and have the skills to help
  • volunteering helps everyone: the non-profits get support for their objectives; junior volunteer gain experience, connections, and advice; senior volunteer give back and develop mentoring skills

Our Projects

Supper Society App

Our first project is with the non-profit, Supper Society, which helps immigrants to Montreal integrate with the local community. We are building a web-based database to match newcomers with established residents and to help established residents host newcomers in their home for a welcome dinner. Get in touch with us to learn more and offer your help.
